# 06 Testing Checklist - Phase 8

This document lists verification procedures for notifications.

---

## 1. Dispatcher Validation
* [ ] As Employee, submit an article for review. Login as Supervisor/Owner and confirm review notification exists.
* [ ] As Supervisor, return an article for revision. Login as Employee and check revision request notification.

---

## 2. Dropdown Actions
* [ ] Click Bell icon to open dropdown panel. Check unread notifications display on top.
* [ ] Click "Mark all as read". Confirm count badge updates to 0.

---

## 3. Preferences Verification
* [ ] Go to My Profile page and disable Article Notifications.
* [ ] Submit another article and confirm no notifications are generated for this user.
