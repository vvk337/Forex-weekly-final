# 04 Revision System - Phase 6

This document logs the revision feedback system details.

---

## 1. Revision Requests
- When a Supervisor selects "Return for Revision" on a pending article:
  - The status is reverted to `DRAFT`.
  - A custom revision comment is injected into the record.

---

## 2. Employee Resubmissions
- Employees see a red `Needs Revision` badge on their draft list.
- Expanding the row reveals the Supervisor's feedback.
- The Employee can edit the draft to correct issues and click "Submit for Review" to resubmit.
