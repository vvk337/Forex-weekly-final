# Employee Guide

As an Employee, your main workflows involve content creation, drafting, and collaborating with team members.

## 1. Creating Draft Publications
- Click **+ New Publication** in your Dashboard.
- Write your Title, Body content, Category, and assign department context.
- Save progress as a `DRAFT` or submit it for review, changing the status to `PENDING` to notify supervisors.

## 2. Direct Messaging & Collaboration
- Click **Inbox** -> **Direct Messages** in the sidebar.
- Click **New DM** to search and initiate chats with peers.
- Check the **Announcements** board for broadcast updates.
- Toggle alert categories in **My Profile** -> **Notification Settings**.
