# 01 Dashboard System - Phase 5

This document details the dynamic, role-specific landing widgets created for the editorial control room dashboard.

---

## 1. Widget Structure by Role

### Employee (Editor/Writer)
- **Stats Card**: My Drafts, My Published Articles, Session Status.
- **My Draft Publications**: List of recent draft articles authored by the user.
- **Quick Actions**: Shortcuts to "+ New Publication" and "Edit My Profile".

### Supervisor (Content Reviewer)
- **Stats Card**: Pending Reviews (total drafts on platform), Supervised Departments list.
- **Pending reviews**: List of drafts from department members ready for approval.
- **Quick Actions**: Shortcuts to "Manage Placements" and "Review Inbox Messages".

### Administrator (Workspace Manager)
- **Stats Card**: Platform Users total, Department count, Active workspaces.
- **Users Directory Preview**: List of recently active users with quick links.
- **Quick Actions**: Shortcut to "Invite New User".

### Owner (System Director)
- Combined landing cards summarizing total platform users, total publications, and active sponsored campaigns.
