# 02 Role Structure

This document details the responsibilities and permissions prepared for the 4 core administrative tiers of the Forex Weekly CMS.

---

## 1. Owner
* **Description**: System Director / Platform Owner.
* **Role Identifier**: `OWNER`
* **Access Scope**: Full administrative capabilities across all workspaces and system settings.
* **Management Capabilities**: Can invite, manage, and archive any user (including Workspace Admins).

---

## 2. Admin
* **Description**: Workspace Administrator.
* **Role Identifier**: `ADMIN`
* **Access Scope**: Full control over settings, campaigns, and user assignments within their authorized workspaces.
* **Management Capabilities**: Can invite and configure roles for Supervisors and Employees within their assigned workspaces.

---

## 3. Supervisor
* **Description**: Content Supervisor / Lead Editor.
* **Role Identifier**: `SUPERVISOR`
* **Access Scope**: Handles article reviews, ticker management, and support inbox inquiries.
* **Management Capabilities**: Directs and reviews drafts created by Employees. Cannot manage user accounts.

---

## 4. Employee
* **Description**: Content Writer / Editor.
* **Role Identifier**: `EMPLOYEE`
* **Access Scope**: Basic publication draft creation and profile editing.
* **Management Capabilities**: None.
