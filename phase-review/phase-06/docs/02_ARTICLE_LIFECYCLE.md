# 02 Article Lifecycle - Phase 6

This document outlines the six core article states:

---

## 1. Article Lifecycle States

1. **Draft**:
   - Newly created articles. Restored or returned revision publications. Editable by the author.

2. **Pending Review**:
   - Submitted articles waiting for Supervisor review and approval.

3. **Published**:
   - Approved articles. Visible on the frontend web application. Read-only for Employees.

4. **Scheduled**:
   - Scheduled publication time set. Automatically transitions to `Published` once the time passes.

5. **Archived**:
   - Closed articles removed from public feeds but remaining searchable.

6. **Trash**:
   - Trashed articles. Restorable by Admin or Owner; otherwise deleted permanently.
